Apex Legends

Learn the legends and weapons as you drop into the new map Stormpoint.

Apex legends is a large scale battle royale with more of twist. being able to play with different legends and their abilities, a vast variety of guns, and in the newest season a new map.

Music

Stormpoint was especially designed to meet the expectations of out player base.

Everyone on the design team was getting comments and see results of teams only landing in high loot areas ending up with 5-10 squads with ring level 2 or even level 1. So in the new map storpoint we designed it to have all of the point of interests towards the outside of the map leading in or where the ring decides to go.
